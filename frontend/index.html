Kyoto, Japan — Places to Visit

A short vacation plan with attractions and activities.

Top Attractions

  • Kiyomizu-dera Temple
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine (thousands of torii gates)
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Gion district and traditional teahouses

Activities

  • Tea ceremony experience
  • Walk the Philosopher’s Path
  • Traditional kaiseki dining
  • Bicycle ride along the Kamo River

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: spring (cherry blossoms) or autumn (fall colors). Use public transport and buy an Icoca card for convenience.
